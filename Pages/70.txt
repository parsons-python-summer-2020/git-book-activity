If God 
Invited you to a party
And said,

"Everyone
In the ballroom tonight
Will be my special
Guest,"

How would you then treat them
When you
Arrived?

Indeed, indeed!

And Hafiz knows
There is not in this world

Who
Is not upon
His Jeweled Dance
Floor.

- Hafiz
